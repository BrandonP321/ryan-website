Mountain Biking Course

In this course we will take you from any skill level to a pro. No need to have even ridden a bike before. Whitewater Rafting

I (Tom) will be teaching all the whitewater rafting courses this summer now that we have Sam taking over the biking courses. In this class we will start on open water practice to get you used to being in the boat first. We will then progress through more challenging rivers at your own pace! Life jackets required!
